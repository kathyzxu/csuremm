Centrality Graphs

Visualizations of different centrality measures on all MTA subway stations, plotted with NetworkX. To aid visual intuition, all centrality measures are linearly scaled from 1 (lowest) to 9 (highest) and color-coded accordingly. Furthermore, station names and distances between stations are removed from maps.
