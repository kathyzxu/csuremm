School Clusters

After identifying the three predominant PCs driving our data, we created two scatter plots of the schools to further investigate centrality-performance PC relationships. Next, we formed three clusters of schools that share similar centrality-performance qualities according to the k-means clustering algorithm (more information about k-means clustering here).

We then categorized each school cluster based on its location on the centrality-performance axes. Curious about the driving performance metrics within each cluster, we ran PCA on the original school data and obtained the following results:
